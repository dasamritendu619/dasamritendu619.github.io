About Us

Tamralipta Sahojog Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to empower marginalized communities through sustainable development initiatives , promoting education, healthcare, and economic opportunities, and fostering social and environmental justice. It was established in the year of 2023 at the historical place Tamluk, Purba Medinipur.

Our Organization is led by the board of governing body including President, Joint Secretaries, Treasurer, Vice-President, Assistant Treasurer, Auditor and Management Committee. The other members are called as General Members.

At Foundation, we are guided by a set of core values including integrity, transparency, etc. These principles form the foundation of our approach to help the poor people in different types of cases such as education, economical matters, hospitality and other necessary things. You can be a part of our mission by volunteer, donation, or supporting us in any situation.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower marginalized communities through sustainable development initiatives, promoting education, healthcare, and economic opportunities, and fostering social and environmental justice, striving towards a world where every individual has access to education, healthcare, and economic opportunities, and where social and environmental equity prevail, fostering thriving and resilient communities.

Our Vision

A world where every individual has access to education, healthcare, and economic opportunities, and where social and environmental equity prevail, fostering thriving and resilient communities.
